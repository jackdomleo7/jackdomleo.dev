About me

Hi, I'm Jack Domleo, a Nottinghamshire-based developer in the UK, currently working as a Frontend Developer at Ocean Finance.

I have 4 and a half years of professional experience working as a developer. I often work with Vue.js, TypeScript, SCSS, Jest and Storybook.

I have a passion for frontend technologies and writing clean websites and applications while maintaining high accessibility standards.

I blog about tech and developer life, have self-published an ebook and love contributing to open-source projects.
